Unveiling Mortality: A Comprehensive Exploration of Causes of Death


This graph shows the mortality rate for specific causes. There are many possible reasons for a person
to perish and unfortunately among that causes is self-harm.


Looking deeper in this visualisation, it could be deduced that the every country in this dataset has a person
die due to self-harm. The number is especially big in China and India.


Based on this chart, we realised that not although India and China has the most casualties due to self-harm,
the United States and Russia are coming in close as well.

It also shows how China has been improving over the years while India is steadily increasing over the years.
